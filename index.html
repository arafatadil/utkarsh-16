ABOUT US


Utkarsh,the largest Hindi cultral fest of North India is one with a motive. Supported by MHRD and organised by ANAMIKA HINDI SOCIETY, the festival was started to promote the importance of Hindi language and with 10 extravagant celebrations,UTKARSH turns 11 this year. Every year the fest has grown and nurtued from plethora of memories, countless dreams and millions of screams. So be a part of something bigger this year, treasure and cherish the aura of joyous moments and witness the colorful blend of culture, drama, music, dance and passion.
